Learning resources for our prospective members

Download instructions

  1. Find the PDF/ MP3/WMAs you require;
  2. Right click on the link with your mouse;
  3. From the pop-up menu, select "Save Target As";
  4. Save the file in an appropriate location on your own computer.

From your computer, you will be able to burn a CD and/or listen to the MP3s/WMAs.

  • If you would like a CD of learning tracks, please tell your section leader which tracks you require. You will be advised of the payment required.

Likewise, you should be able to view and/or print your sheet music (PDF).

  • If you have problem downloading music (or if what you want isn't here), please e-mail Hilary Gladstone.
